# Business recommendations
##
- The business main focus item should be salads.
- They are recommended to promote salads more through various marketing methods like free sample, campaigns, collaborations, etc.
- Customer mostly prefer in-store. So their in-store experience should be priority.
- The working of cafe should be smooth as well as efficient and facilities in store should be maintained and upgraded too.
- Customer mostly prefer cash as a way of payment method but credit card is not ignored too.
- Hence, both should be managed in such a way that customer do not face inconvenience while paying.
